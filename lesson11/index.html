Friday Tree Club
By: Art Phillips

Do you love trees? Do you enjoy going to parks and trying your hand at identifying your local maples? If you do, you should come join the local Tree Club! We spend our Friday afternoons exploring our local parks and reserves for trees of all species. After our meetings, we love to discuss all things tree! Feel free to bring a snack to share! Meetings start at 2:00p.m. and go to 4:00p.m. All ages welcome!
