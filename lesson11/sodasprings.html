Local area trail activities!

By: Phil Roberts

Walking on trails flocked with dew-kissed flowers and towering trees can bring peace and quiet to a overworked mind. Individuals and families can both benefit from a good hike, but need some motivation to get out there? How about a cup of free hot cocoa waiting for you at the end of the trail? Christmas Season Hike starts at your local trail Dec. 21 at 5:00 p.m. . Lovers' Date Night Hike offers a chance to purchase sweets at the end of the trail for your one and only! Valentines' Day at 5:00 sharp! These are just a few fun activities to help bring our community out to enjoy what nature has to offer! Happy hiking!
