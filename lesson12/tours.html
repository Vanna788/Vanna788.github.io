Tours

Cozumel is an island of history and beauty. Cozumel was once inhabited by up to 10,000 Mayans. The island has been visited by many Conquistadors and pirates, which caused the number of Mayans to decrease due to disease and attack. Tourist and locals can still see the ruins of what is left of the past. The center of the island, San Gervasio, contains the most ruins.
Cozumel is home to some of the most beautiful coral reefs on the planet! The coast also contains a large number of different fish and other marine life. Don't forget to pack or rent subadiving gear! The coast is bound to leave your breathless!
Cozumel offers a variety of activities for all ages. One of these is horseback riding on the coast! On the coast right outside on the center of the downtown, you will have the opportunity to choose from day, night , or an all day tour along the coast! Tours may very due to weather.
