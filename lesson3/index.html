The site purpose

Cloudy Corner aims to bring its viewers a website that provides local weather infomation that is easy to navigate and understand. Cloudy Corner will also provide users with weather stories from their local areas, as well as current weather conditions for their town. Whether they use a smartphone, tablet, or home computer, Our site will serve anyone on any device.
