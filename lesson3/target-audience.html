Target Audience

The target audience will be users ages 14+. They will be from various educational backgrounds. They will also have various levels of knowlege in meteorology. The users will use the website when they are planning outdoor activites, reserching the outcome of present weather conditions, and weather spotting for local areas. Users will visit the site using their smartphones, personal computers, and tablets.

Two personas

Robert Morris

  • Job Title: Retired High School Science Teacher
  • Demographics: Mr. Morris is 60, married, and has two children and three grandchildren. He has a bachelor's degree in Science.
  • Goals and Site Motivation: Mr. Morris is a weather enthusiast and has a weather blog where he uploads photos and videos of various weather phenomena. He will use the Cloudy Corner site on his smartphone to find inclement weather in his area, and to read articles relating to weather.
  • Social: Mr. Morris enjoys taking his wife and grandchildren to National Parks to camp and view wildlife. He also enjoys having cook-outs with old friends.
  • Technology use: Mr. Morris is a licensed HAM operator and has photography skills. He mainly uses his smartphone, but also uses his personal computer to update his weather blog.
  • Quote: "Your never too old to stop learning!"

Tamara Larson

  • Job Title: Youth Softball Coach
  • Demographics: Ms. Larson is 30 years old. She is married and has four children. She has a certificate in Physical Education. She taught P.E. at a local elementry school for 5 years.
  • Goals and Site Motivation: Ms. Larson is the head coach of her team. She is in charge of teaching, planning, and taking care of offical team business. She will use the Cloudy Corner website on her smartphone and Personal Computer to plan games on days clear of bad weather, and to monitor weather conditions during games.
  • Social: Ms. Larson likes to do diy projects on the weekend and take fitness classes with friends. She goes on a monthly hiking trip with her family.
  • Technology use: Ms. Larson prefers to use her Personal Computer to take care of team business. The majority of the time she uses her smartphone.
  • Quote: "I keep my priorities straight. Organization is key."

Five scenarios

  • Should I plan baseball practice on Tuesday? Will the weather be clear?
  • Will I need to take my umbrella to work with me today?
  • Is the thunderstorm going to pass over my town? Should I stay home?
  • Will the storm stop before class is over? Should I call for a ride home?
  • How bad was the wind in the next town over last night?
