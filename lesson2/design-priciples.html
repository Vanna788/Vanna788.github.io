Whitespace and clean design

Walmart

www.walmart.com

Walmart's website is a good example of utilizing whitespace. The images are spaced in such a way that allows more then one to fit nicely on the page, without making it feel cluttered and closed in. This in turn creates an open and clean looking website.

Repetition

D&D Beyond

www.dndbeyond.com

D&D Beyond's website has a good example of repetition in their repeated use of the red font color from the logo for the author of an article. They also reuse the white font color in the article dates, as well as the background color of the site, also taken from the logo.

Contrast

Scentsy

www.scentsy.com

I think Scentsy's website is a good example of contrast mainly due to their plain white background. Their various products seem to pop out againt it, which allows for the viewer to see the item, details and all, without distraction.
